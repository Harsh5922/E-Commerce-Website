About Us

Vasan Eye Care Hospital, Majura Gate, Surat is part of The Vasan Eye Care network which has a number of eye care hospitals in India and abroad. Vasan Eye Care has introduced various ophthalmic technologies and has a dedicated team of qualified surgeons who are specialists in various fields of ophthalmology. All their hospitals are well-equipped with the state-of-the-art facilities and they use cutting-edge technology for all their procedures. Vasan Eye Care offers Cataract Services, Refractive Surgery Services, Cornea Services, Glaucoma Services, Retina Services, Paediatric Ophthalmology and Squint Services, Oculoplasty Services, UVEITIS, Neuro-Ophthalmology, Vision Rehabilitation Services, Computer Vision Syndrome Clinic, Contact Lenses and Optical Services.
