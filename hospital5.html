About Us

Nirmal Hospital has increased its capacity to 150 beds in the same premises by constructing new building. In addition, Nirmal Hospital has added four Operation Theaters, Surgical ICU, Dialysis Unit, Dental Department, Computer Assisted Surgery (CAS) Navigation System for joint replacement surgery and even planning to install CT Scan in near future. Nirmal Hospital with humanitarian is already recognized for its unique blend of superior medical treatment combined with special care.
