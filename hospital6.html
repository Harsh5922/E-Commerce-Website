About Us

Shri B.D. Mehta Mahavir Heart Institute is a state of the art 110 beds super speciality cardiac institute situated in Surat, Gujarat. Shri B.D. Mehta Mahavir Heart Institute is run by the Shree Mahavir Health and Relief Society, a charitable trust. The mission of the trust and the hospital is to give affordable and high-quality cardiac services to the people.
